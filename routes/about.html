About us

Our Story

Foundation stone was laid 136 Years ago in '1884' as the Landmark year for Shri Vaishnav Group of Institutions. Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Trust believes in taking the nation forward by improving the quality of life, working in the sphere of education, health and environment. It has been established to promote education and research in various disciplines through academic institutions for benefits of all sections of the Society, but not with the motive of Profit. 'Only those who will risk going too far can possibly find out how far one can go' From 1981 , 'Shri Vaishnav Sahayak Kapada Market Committee' is running the following Trusts, Colleges, Schools and Institutes of Professional Studies and Research also rendering social service!!
